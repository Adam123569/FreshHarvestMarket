Our Journey Begins

Fresh Harvest Market was born from a simple dream: to reconnect families with the food they eat. Founded by the Rodriguez family, who have been farmers for three generations, we saw a growing disconnect between consumers and the source of their food.

What started as a small local farm stand has grown into a mission-driven marketplace. We believe in supporting local farmers, promoting sustainable agriculture, and delivering the highest quality, freshest produce possible.
